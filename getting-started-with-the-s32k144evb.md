### 1.3 Understanding the HMI Mapping

|Component|S32K144|
|-|-|
|Red LED|PTD15 (FTM0 CH0)|
|Blue LED|PTD0 (FTM0 CH2)|
|Green LED|PTD16 (FTM0 CH1)|
|Potentiometer|PTD14 (ADC0_SE12)|
|SW2|PTC12|
|SW3|PTC13|
